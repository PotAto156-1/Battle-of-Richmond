Why were many of the Union soldiers fighting at Richmond inexperienced or "green"?

As the Confederates moved into Kentucky, states like Illinois, Indiana, and Ohio quickly sent inexperienced, or "green," soldiers to the front lines. They were concerned that if Kentucky fell, their own states might be next to face invasion.
