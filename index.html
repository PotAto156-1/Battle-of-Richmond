Madison County, KY Aug 29 - 30, 1862

How it Ended

The Battle of Richmond ended in a confederate victory. After a running fight, the battle ended when the Confederate forces overran the Unions in the city of Richmond. Only a few Unions escaped, while hundreds were captured. Gen. William “Bull” Nelson managed to escape and was part of the few lucky ones. This battle opened the state of Kentucky to the Confederacy and also gave a much needed boost to bring the state under confederate control. On September 2nd, the capital of Kentucky, Frankfort, fell under Confederate control and was the only capital the Union lost during the Civil War.
