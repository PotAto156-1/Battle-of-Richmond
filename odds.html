Odds and Outcomes

13,350 people engaged in the battle, with 6850 Union troops and 6500 Confederate troops. The Union troops had a slight edge against the Confederates, but it was almost even. There were 5,650 casualties, 4,900 of which were Union troops while only 750 of them were Confederate troops. This clearly shows that the Confederates won this war.
