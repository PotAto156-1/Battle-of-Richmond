After the Battle

Out of the 6,800 Union soldiers who fought at Richmond, about 4,000 were taken prisoner. The rest were either killed, wounded, or managed to escape toward Lexington. Just a few days later, on September 2, Confederate forces under Smith captured Frankfort, making it the only Union state capital to fall during the Civil War.
